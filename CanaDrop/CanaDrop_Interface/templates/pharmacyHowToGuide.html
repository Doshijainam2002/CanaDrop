How To Guide

A step-by-step guide to start, manage, and track deliveries

Welcome to CanaLogistiX Delivery Services

Your trusted and reliable delivery partner. This guide will help you navigate the platform from first login to invoicing.

  • Navigate to the Pharmacy Login page.
  • Enter your registered email address.
  • Enter the temporary password provided by CanaLogistiX Delivery Services from the Registration Email.
  • Update your password upon your first successful login from "My Profile".
  • Security Note: Your account is secured and accessible only to authorized pharmacy staff.

Your dashboard provides a real-time summary of your order activity and billing information.

  • Total Orders: Total number of orders placed with CanaLogistiX Delivery Services.
  • Pending Orders: Orders that have not yet been accepted by a delivery partner.
  • Completed Orders: Orders that have been successfully delivered.
  • Weekly Due: Estimated weekly charges for completed orders (refer to invoices for final amounts).
  • Recent Orders: A quick view of your most recent orders and their key details.

The order form includes Required Fields (must be completed to place an order) and Optional Fields (can be provided if applicable).

Required Fields

  • Customer Name
  • Customer Phone
  • Pickup Address and Pickup City are auto-populated based on your registered business address (can be updated if required)
  • Pickup Day
  • Drop City and Drop Address
  • Rate is automatically calculated based on distance and other factors prior to submission

Optional Fields

  • Signature Verification: Collects the customer’s signature and generates a signed acknowledgement PDF
  • ID Verification: Requires the delivery partner to verify identification before handing over the medication
  • Alternate Contact Number: Used if the primary phone number is not reachable
  • Delivery Notes: Any special instructions for the delivery partner

Note

  • Medications are handed directly to the customer and are not left at the door, reception, or with another individual.
  • If a delivery is unsuccessful (for example, the customer is unavailable), the medications are returned to the pharmacy. If the pharmacy is closed, the return is completed on the next business day.
  • If you are unable to create an order and receive an error indicating the pharmacy is inactive, contact an administrator. A common reason is overdue invoice payment.
  • The handover process begins once the order status changes to Accepted (a delivery partner has accepted the order).
  • On the pickup day, request the delivery partner’s Driver ID and verify it against the Driver ID and Driver Image shown for the order on the Orders page.
  • Once verified, write the Order Number on the sealed package, take a clear front photo of the sealed packet for each order, and upload it under Pharmacy Handover in the order section on the Orders page. After the handover image is uploaded, the order status will update to Picked Up.
  • After receiving the sealed package, the delivery partner will upload the Driver Pickup Image, which will be visible to the pharmacy on the Orders page. Once the driver pickup image is uploaded, the order status will update to In Transit.
  • The delivery partner completes the delivery based on the order requirements and uploads the final Drop-off Image, which will be visible on the Orders page. If ID Verification is selected, the verification status will be shown. If Signature Verification is selected, the signed customer acknowledgement PDF will be available. At completion, the order will be marked as Delivered.
  • Status timestamps are also displayed on the Orders page to show when each step was completed.
  • Delivery tracking is available from the Orders page by opening the respective order view.
  • Each order displays a complete delivery timeline showing all status changes (Accepted, Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered) along with corresponding timestamps.
  • Delivery proof images are available for review, including pharmacy handover images, driver pickup images, and final drop-off images.
  • If ID Verification is selected, the verification status is clearly shown within the order details.
  • If Signature Verification is selected, the signed customer acknowledgement (ACK) PDF is available for download directly from the order view.
  • This information supports compliance requirements, and audit reviews by providing full visibility into each delivery.
  • ID Verification ensures that medications are handed directly to the intended customer.
  • When ID Verification is selected, the delivery partner verifies the customer’s identification (without retaining personal ID details) before completing the delivery.
  • The verification status is recorded and displayed within the order details on the Orders page.
  • Signature Verification requires the customer to provide a signature at the time of delivery.
  • When Signature Verification is selected, a signed customer acknowledgement (ACK) PDF is automatically generated and attached to the order.
  • Both verification methods support regulatory compliance, audit readiness, and delivery accountability.
  • An order can be cancelled only when its status is Pending or Accepted. Once the order progresses beyond this stage, cancellation is no longer permitted.
  • If an order was created with incorrect information, do not attempt to cancel the order. Instead, request updates by raising a Contact Admin ticket to modify the order details.
  • Direct order cancellation is not available from the Orders page. Cancellation requests must be initiated from the Contact Admin section and require a valid reason.
  • Upon submitting a cancellation request, an administrator will review and respond within 24–48 hours. Orders will only be cancelled if they are still in Pending or Accepted status.
  • Once an order has been Picked Up, In Transit, or Delivered, cancellation is not possible, even by an administrator, due to chain-of-custody and compliance requirements.
  • After an order moves beyond Pending and Accepted, charges may still apply even if the delivery is not completed due to pharmacy-side issues or non-compliance during the handover process. In such cases, the order charges may still appear on the invoice.
  • Invoices are generated on a weekly basis and include only successfully delivered orders completed during that billing period.
  • It is the pharmacy’s responsibility to review the Invoices section each week to confirm when invoices are generated and to note the applicable due date.
  • Once an invoice is generated, it will appear in the Invoices section along with a downloadable invoice document and the payment due date.
  • The recommended payment method is card payment through the portal, which is processed instantly. Other payment methods may also be available and are listed on the invoice.
  • If payment is not received by the invoice due date, the pharmacy account may be temporarily suspended, and new orders cannot be created until the outstanding balance is fully paid.
  • For support, submit a Contact Admin ticket from the portal.
  • Select the appropriate issue category, provide a clear description of the problem, and submit the ticket.
  • Include relevant details such as the Order Number, current order status, and any related notes to help expedite resolution.
  • An administrator will review the request and respond within 24–48 hours via your pharmacy’s registered email address.
  • Ensure all orders are created with accurate customer and address details to avoid delays or failed deliveries.
  • Prepare and securely seal packages before the delivery partner arrives, and keep orders ready on the scheduled pickup day.
  • Clearly write the Order Number on each sealed package prior to handover.
  • Always verify the delivery partner’s Driver ID and Driver Image against the order details before handing over medications.
  • Use ID Verification and Signature Verification options where appropriate to meet compliance and audit requirements.
  • Regularly monitor the Orders and Invoices sections to track order status, verify delivery completion, and ensure timely payments.
  • Review invoices weekly and complete payments before the due date to avoid account suspension and service interruptions.
  • For any issues related to orders, billing, or account status, promptly raise a Contact Admin ticket with complete details to enable faster resolution.
