How To Guide

A step-by-step guide to start, manage, and track deliveries

Welcome to CanaLogistiX Delivery Services

Your local prescription and product delivery partner. This guide will help you navigate the platform from first login to invoicing.

Visit the Pharmacy Login Page and enter your registered email and temporary password provided by CanaLogistiX Delivery Services.

Your dashboard displays active orders, order status, assigned drivers, delivery history, and invoices at a glance.

Click "Create New Order" and fill in customer name, phone, pickup address, drop address, and select compliance options like signature or ID verification.

When the driver arrives, verify their identity, hand over the package, and the driver will upload a handover photo for proof.

Track every status change, who performed the action, timestamps, and proof images for patient follow-ups and compliance audits.

Drivers may request customer ID and capture signature confirmation to meet regulatory requirements and pharmacy audit standards.

Orders can only be cancelled when in Pending or Accepted status. Once picked up or in transit, cancellation is not allowed due to chain of custody.

Invoices are generated weekly (Mondayâ€“Sunday) with one consolidated invoice including total deliveries, per-delivery rate, and invoice PDF.

Go to Support / Contact Admin, select a subject, and submit your message for order issues, delivery delays, invoice questions, or technical issues.

Prepare packages before driver arrival, clearly label patient name and address, and use ID verification for controlled medications.
