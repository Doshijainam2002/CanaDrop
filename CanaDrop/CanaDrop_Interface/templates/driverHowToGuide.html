How To Guide

Complete workflow guide for delivery partners

Welcome to CanaLogistiX Delivery Partner Program

Your complete guide to successful deliveries - from onboarding to earnings and compliance.

Before accessing delivery opportunities, you must complete onboarding as an Independent Contractor.

Required Documents

  • Government-issued ID
  • Driver's License
  • Vehicle Insurance
  • Vehicle Ownership / Registration
  • Proof of Right to Work in Canada
  • Front-facing profile photo (My Identity section)

Identity Photo Requirement

  • A clear, front-facing photo is mandatory
  • Pharmacies may require visual identity verification before handing over medications
  • If your identity photo is missing or unclear, pharmacies may refuse to release packages

Required Agreements

  • Independent Contractor Agreement
  • Privacy / PHIPA & Confidentiality Agreement

New Delivery Notifications

  • Drivers receive email notifications when new deliveries are posted
  • Regularly check the Dashboard in the portal
  • Dashboard displays all pending deliveries, pickup pharmacy details, pickup day, and store operating hours

Note: Compliance steps (Signature / ID) are not shown at the acceptance stage. Full requirements appear only after accepting the delivery.

Accepting a Delivery

  • Deliveries are not auto-assigned - drivers choose which orders to accept
  • Once accepted, the delivery is reserved for that driver
  • The driver becomes responsible for completing it
  • Accept only deliveries you can complete within pharmacy hours

Most partner businesses (including pharmacies) operate during standard business hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed (in most cases)

Driver Responsibility

  • Review store timing details shown in the portal before accepting a delivery
  • Not all pharmacies follow identical schedules
  • Pickup and return attempts must always respect store hours

Warning: Accepting a delivery without confirming store hours may result in unsuccessful pickups, delays, required returns, and reduced future delivery visibility.

At the Pharmacy

  • Arrive during pharmacy business hours
  • Show your identity and ID number from the "My Identity" section
  • Confirm you are the assigned driver
  • Verify that the order number is written on the package and matches the delivery details

Pickup Proof

  • Take a pickup photo at the pharmacy for the respective order
  • This photo confirms custody transfer

After Pickup

  • Secure the package
  • Proceed directly to the delivery location

Signature & ID Verification

  • Signature and ID requirements are explicitly shown per order
  • If not required, proceed normally
  • If signature is required: ask the customer for signature, tap "Save Signature", then hand over medications
  • For ID verification: ask for any valid ID, verify the name matches the order, then complete the handoff

Customer Unavailable

  • Call the customer and ask for instructions
  • If instructed to leave the package: leave at door/reception/concierge only if verbally authorized
  • If unreachable: call the pharmacy for guidance and follow pharmacy instructions if verbally authorized
  • If no contact is established: return medications to the pharmacy and take a return photo as delivery proof (you will still be paid)

Completing the Delivery (In Order)

  1. Save signature / complete ID verification (if required)
  2. Add driver notes (if needed)
  3. Take final drop-off photo
  4. Upload photo — this automatically completes the delivery

Raise a support ticket for any unexpected issue.

Medication Returns

  • Medications must be returned to the original pharmacy
  • If the pharmacy is closed: return on the next business day

Important Safety Rules

  • Never leave medications unless explicitly authorized
  • Do not leave packages at doors, with neighbors, with unauthorized staff, or in unsecured locations

Cancellation Process

  • Cancellations must be done by raising a support ticket
  • Clearly state the reason and mention the order ID

Allowed Cancellation Window

  • Cancellations are allowed up to 2 hours before pharmacy closing time

Warning: Repeated late cancellations may impact driver access to future deliveries.

Payment Structure

  • Drivers are paid bi-weekly
  • Earnings are calculated per completed delivery

Driver Invoices

  • Drivers will receive invoices for their completed deliveries
  • Invoices show delivery breakdown, and net payable amount

Payment Method

  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)
  • Drivers must provide: Institution number, Transit number, Account number, and authorization to store banking details
  • Every successful delivery earns 50 CC Points
  • Points are currently banked for future use

Future Uses

  • Merchandise purchases
  • Partial cash payouts
  • Promotions and rewards

Referrals & Performance Incentives

  • CanaLogistiX regularly offers referrals and performance-based incentives
  • Drivers who complete more successful deliveries may see more delivery opportunities
  • May become eligible for higher-paying deliveries and incremental earning increases

Drivers Must

  • Be punctual and professional
  • Communicate respectfully
  • Follow pharmacy instructions
  • Drive safely

Package Responsibility

  • Once medications are handed to the driver, the driver is fully responsible
  • Loss, damage, or mishandling is the driver's responsibility

Zero Tolerance Policy

  • Privacy violations
  • Medication mishandling
  • Fraud
  • Unsafe conduct

Drivers handle Personal Health Information (PHI) and must:

  • Keep all information confidential
  • Use data only for delivery completion
  • Never store or share customer information

Drivers acknowledge that they are independent contractors. CanaLogistiX does not:

  • Withhold taxes
  • Provide benefits
  • Reimburse expenses (unless stated)

Drivers Are Responsible For

  • Taxes
  • Insurance
  • Vehicle costs
  • Compliance with laws

Raise support tickets for:

  • Delivery issues
  • Cancellations
  • Compliance problems

Do not resolve disputes directly with customers - always contact support.
